About Lucas!

I am a doctoral researcher at UCL Queen Square Institute of Neurology, University College London, working under supervision of Prof. Matthew Walker and Dr Yujiang Wang (Newcastle University). My work consists of studying seizure emergence in electrophysiological signals with complex systems approaches. I also have interest in general data analysis and machine learning.
